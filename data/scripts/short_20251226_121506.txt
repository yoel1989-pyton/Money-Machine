Did you know a secretive entity controls your money? 

The Federal Reserve, or the Fed, is the U.S. central bank. It influences interest rates, controls inflation, and regulates the money supply. This hidden system shapes our economy. 

Since 2008, the Fed's balance sheet has soared from $800 billion to over $8 trillion! That’s a massive impact on your purchasing power. 

But here’s the twist: the Fed's decisions often benefit big banks more than you. It’s a complicated game, and the average person is left in the dark.
