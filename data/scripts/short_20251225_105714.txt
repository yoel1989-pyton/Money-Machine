Why is saving money making you poorer? 

Most people think saving is smart. But there's a hidden system at play. Money loses value over time. Inflation eats away at your savings. 

Consider this: In the last decade, inflation has averaged over 2% a year. If you saved $10,000, you lost buying power. It’s like watching your money vanish! 

Here’s the twist: Banks profit off your savings. They lend it out, while you earn barely anything. 

Don’t let your hard-earned cash sit idle. Invest it instead!
