Is the middle class vanishing? Let’s dive in!

In fact, since 1971, the middle class shrank from 61% to just 50% of the population. That’s millions of families struggling! 

But here’s the twist: It’s not just about economics. Social mobility is at an all-time low. Kids aren’t surpassing their parents anymore.
