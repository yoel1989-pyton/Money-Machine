### The Zero Inbox Method

**Rule 1: Check at Scheduled Times**
- Morning: 9 AM (30 min max)
- Afternoon: 2 PM (30 min max)
- Never first thing in morning

**Rule 2: 2-Minute Rule**
- Can you respond in under 2 minutes?
- Yes â†’ Do it now
- No â†’ Convert to task with deadline

**Rule 3: One-Touch Processing**
Every email gets one of four actions:
1. **Delete** - Not needed
2. **Delegate** - Forward to right person
3. **Do** - Under 2 minutes
4. **Defer** - Create task for later

**Rule 4: Template Everything**
- Create templates for common responses
- Use text expander (TextExpander, Espanso)
- Personalize only what needs it
