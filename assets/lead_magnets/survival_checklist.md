## INTRODUCTION

When disaster strikes, you have 72 hours before help arrives. This checklist ensures you're prepared for anything.

**The Rule of 3:**
- 3 minutes without air
- 3 hours without shelter (in extreme conditions)
- 3 days without water
- 3 weeks without food

### Minimum: 1 gallon per person per day

- [ ] **Water Storage** (minimum 3 gallons per person)
  - [ ] Water containers (BPA-free)
  - [ ] Water purification tablets
  - [ ] Portable water filter (LifeStraw or Sawyer)
  
- [ ] **Water Sources Knowledge**
  - [ ] Know nearest natural water sources
